—— Why Me ——

How my experiences, strengths, and philosophy translate into being your best hire ever

Perspective

My first two years in the technology industry were spent in a project management role. Having managed the development process, I have the skills to self-direct with sensitivity toward budget and timeline considerations and the project's big picture. My extended perspective on the holistic process of shipping a product make me so much more than simply a code monkey.

Mover and Shaker

As an undergraduate at Brown University, I established my own major, Language & Culture Studies, to tailor my curriculum and maximize its value. When working a project manager in Chicago inspired a career transition to coding, I dropped everything to move to the heart of the tech scene and study at Dev Bootcamp, equipping myself with new skills and direction. I have a track record of taking action to achieve my goals, never being stoppped by fears or challenges.

Agility

As a polyglot, I understand how to derive patterns to facilitate learning. I started my studies with French, so by the time I added Spanish, I skipped two levels because I didn't need to start with verb moods and the types of articles. After Latin, declensions in Russian were a breeze, and Polish is awfully similar to Russian if you know what pronunciation tweaks to make. This dexterity allows me to pick up new technologies with ease.

Attitude

I am the lighthearted cheer during an all-nighter, the voice of optimism in the face of seemingly insurmountable challenges, the sharer of homemade cookies on a Monday. Seriously, I bake lots of cookies and like to share.

Information Hoarder

I am a collector of information. When I have a meeting, I show up with all my ducks in a row and every material read and annotated so that we can get right down to business.

Communication

Words are my trade. I strive for the trifecta of clarity, concision, and kindness. I am an active listener and believe that empathetic communication can solve the majority of conflict that teams face.

—— About Me ——

Beyond being a rockstar dev, I'm also a human who likes things like pancakes and pugs!

Originally from Nebraska, I made my way to San Francisco by way of Providence and Chicago, with some stints in France and Russia along the way. Some fun facts: my patronus is a pug, my fatal flaw is hoarding too many browser tabs, my favorite book is The Little Prince (in the original French, bien sûr), and my go-to accessory is a weird hat. I make some really amazing lemon-filled brownies (you're skeptical now but wait until I bring some to work to share). In my free time, I can be found surfing the Web's vegetarian blogs and feminist sites, frolicking at farmers' markets, or blowing off steam at yoga.
