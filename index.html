—— Why Me ——

How my experiences, strenghts, and philosophy translate into being your best hire ever

Organization

My first two years in the technology industry were spent in a project management role. I know the right questions to ask, the risks to heed, challenges to consider, and pitfalls to preempt. Moreover, having managed a tech team, I have the skills to manage myself. I am hyper aware of budget and timeline restrictions and take seriously the factors driving a project beyond the technical role I play.

Intellection

My brain is always going. I am driven by a relentless curiosity to leave no stone unturned in pursuing knowledge. I seek the next, the best, the latest. An agile methodology comes pre-programmed. I surround myself with smart people and revel in the particular joy of synergy of knowledge. I never shy away from the tricky conversations or the hard problems because finding the answers and untangling the clutter makes my eyes light up.

Attitude

I am the lighthearted cheer during an all-nighter, the voice of optimism in the face of seemingly insurmountable challenges, the presenter of homemade cookies on a Monday. Seriously, I bake lots of cookies.

Input

am a collector of information. Browser tabs full of StackOverflow topics and new recipes and articles from Twitter and inspiring websites to pin, my emails neatly sorted into folders and subfolders with nary a message out of place, sticky notes with new tricks and questions and reminders. When I have a meeting, I show up with all my ducks in a row and every material read and annotated so that we can get right down to business.

Adaptability

Ready to change course at a moment’s notice, if called for. Information gets inputted, triaged, and handled.

Compassion

As a social justice warrior in my free time, I endeavor to infuse all my dealings with understanding and humanity. With my timeless idealism, I’m truly here to make the world a better place.

Connectedness

Perspective

Communication

Words are my trade. I strive for the trifecta of clarity, concision, and kindness in my interactions with others. I am an active listener and believe that compassionate communication can solve the majority of conflict that teams face.

—— About Me ——

Beyond being a rockstar dev, I'm also a human who likes things like pancakes and pugs!

Originally from Nebraska, I've lived in four states and four countries. I speak, to varying fluencies, French, Russian, Spanish, Polish, and Latin. My spirit animal is a pug. My go-to news source is the New York Times, go-to pick-me-up is Humans of New York, and go-to accessory is a weird hat. I make really amazing lemon-filled brownies (I know it sounds weird but just go with it). Farmers' markets make me giddy, and I believe that true perfection lies in the optimally ripe avocado. When not working, I can be found surfing the Web's vegetarian blogs and feminist sites, making a mess in the kitchen, or listening to tearful singer-songwriters. Fun fact: My favorite book is The Little Prince (in the original French, bien sûr.
